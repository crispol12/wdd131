Sidharta School of Singing - Site Plan

Organization and planning of the website for the singing academy.

Site Name

Sidharta School of Singing

This name represents an academy dedicated to teaching singing and vocalization techniques, inspired by personal and artistic growth. It is easy to remember and captures the essence of the academy.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide detailed information about classes, schedules, events, and resources for learning to sing. It will also include a registration form for new students and news about upcoming events.

Scenarios

  • What are the upcoming events and concerts organised by the academy?
  • How can I sign up for advanced singing classes?

Color Schema

Selected Colors:

  • Dark Blue (#1a2a6c): Header and footer background.
  • light golden (#ffcc33): Accents and buttons.
  • White (#ffffff): Text in dark areas for good contrast.
  • Dark Grey(#4b4b4b): H2 Titles .

Typography

Selected Fonts:

  • Montserrat: Headings and titles for their professional and clear appearance.
  • Roboto: Body text for easy reading.
