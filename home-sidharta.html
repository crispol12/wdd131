Sidharta, a place where you will find your talent, near you in Otavalo-Ecuador.

The Sidharta singing school is the result of a life of singing. Its owner, Christian Castillo, has been singing since he was 6 years old. At 37, he is now in a stage of maturity that allows him to share his experience with people who wish to reach their full potential in singing.

About Us

Welcome to Sidharta Singing School! We are dedicated to helping individuals unlock their full potential in singing. Founded by Christian Castillo, who has over 30 years of singing experience, our school offers a nurturing environment where passion for music and voice meets professional training.

Our mission is to guide our students in finding their unique voices and to inspire them to achieve their musical dreams. Located in Otavalo, Ecuador, we are excited to welcome aspiring singers from all backgrounds.
