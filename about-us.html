Welcome to Sidharta Singing School

We are dedicated to helping individuals unlock their full potential in singing. Founded by Christian Castillo, who has over 30 years of singing experience, our school offers a nurturing environment where passion for music and voice meets professional training.

Sidharta is a school made for anyone who wants to improve their vocal technique or simply wants to learn singing to relax. Come and share with us, life is too short to do things you don't like. Enjoy the greatest expression of truth within you, your singing.
